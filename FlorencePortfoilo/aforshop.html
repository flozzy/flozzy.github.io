Karlskrona Afroshop

I had the opportunity to work with the owners of the Karlskrona Afroshop, to design their new website and develop it. As a UX Designer, I developed high-fidelity wireframes, clickable prototypes, and user flows for the website to enhance user engagement. The website is meant to help people see the types of products that are available at the shop. It will also assist them to find the shop location and contact information. For now the owners asked that there shouldn't be any payment on the website. To go to the website click Here
