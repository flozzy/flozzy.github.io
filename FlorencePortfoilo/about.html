Hey, I'm Florence - a designer and developer hoping to further my career in the front-end space.

I was born in Imo State, Nigeria and grew up in Lobatse, Botswana. Growing up, I had a unique perspective of the world - being Nigerian in a foreign land. I learned everyone has a story to tell, and it's important to be open to new changes and fresh ideas.

I found the love for designing and developing in my second year of University back in Russia. I graduated from Lobachevsky State University of Nizhny Novgorod , with a degree in information Technology.

Currently, I'm a master's student at the Blekinge institute of technology with specialisation in frontend development, content collaboration softwares and photo & video editing. I design new User interface for applications/websites and also build new websites.

A talented and creative designer who is a self-motivated, energetic, enthusiastic, and aspiring junior front-end developer. I enjoy practicing and building projects to learn new skills. Expertise in  HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

I am passionate about improving the lives of others through design and development. As a designer, I create value-adding products and services. To do this, I apply design thinking and a human-centered design approach spanning from running user studies, design research, and conceptualizing ideas to designing prototypes, building business models - all while keeping the people at the center. Outside of designing and developing, I like to travel, explore new places and meet new people. I go hiking sometimes and my fun fact is that I can speak four Languages.
