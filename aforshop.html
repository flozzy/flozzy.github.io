Karlskrona Afroshop

I had the opportunity to work with the owns of the Karlskrona Afroshop, to design their new website. As a UX Designer, I developed high-fidelity wireframes, clickable prototypes, and user flows for the website to enhance user engagement. The website is meant to help people see the types of products that are available with their prices at the shop. it will also assist them on the shop location and contact information.



    LandingPage

    This is what a user sees when they first load the page. It shows the types of categories and the 2 different section of the shop. I also call this the wecoming page.


    Salon Landing Page

    This is the landing page for the salon section of the shop. To the left the user can see all the categories fo rthe salon section.




    Product list page.

    This page is an example of a category product listing. It shows the different types of products that are available for that category. It also shows their prices, name and weight of the product.



    Product View

    This is view of the product.
    This shows all the product information such as product name, price, sizes that are available, the nutritional values and they where it was manufactured.



    Location Page

    This is Location Page.
    This helps people to locate the shop and it shows the days and hours the shop is open.



    Prototyping

    This shows how the website is meant to function.
